Privacy & POPIA Notice

Last updated: March 2026 — DUT Collaboration Hub — Group 14

This application is developed as an academic project by students of the Durban University of Technology (DUT). It complies with the principles of the Protection of Personal Information Act, No. 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

1. Who We Are

The DUT Collaboration Hub is a web-based platform developed by Group 14 (PBDV301 & PBDE401) to facilitate faculty-student collaborative project management at DUT.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

When you register and use this platform, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address (used as your login credential)
  • Department / faculty affiliation
  • Role (student or faculty)
  • Project applications, submissions, and messages you create within the platform
  • Files you upload to the platform

3. Why We Collect Your Information

Your personal information is collected for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account on the platform
  • To facilitate project collaboration between students and faculty
  • To send notifications related to your projects, applications, and workshops
  • To track academic progress and milestone submissions

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are securely hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Data is stored in a secure relational database
  • Access to your data is role-based — only authorised users can view relevant information
  • Session management is handled securely to prevent unauthorised access

5. Who Has Access to Your Information

  • Faculty members can see applications submitted to their projects and student names/emails of enrolled students
  • Administrators can view user accounts for platform management purposes
  • Your personal information is not shared with third parties outside this platform

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to the processing of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact your system administrator.

7. Cookies and Sessions

This application uses session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies are essential for the platform to function and do not track you across other websites.

8. Changes to This Notice

This privacy notice may be updated as the platform evolves. Any significant changes will be communicated via the platform's notification system.

9. Contact

This platform is an academic project. For any privacy-related concerns, please speak to your DUT lecturer or module coordinator for PBDV301 / PBDE401.
